Web Security

How would you advise a potential client to secure his/her website from web security attacks? Note: the word count for your answer should not be greater than 250 words

First of all we have to agree to cover all the steps that are in our power to ensure security of the website. We will do that with practises that are proven efficient, among which are: ensuring that the data that is accepted by system is in the right format. After that we have to ensure authorization and authentication are hard to crack. As well important is to ensure that a third party application is trusted and tested. We also have to take in consideration users that leave their credentials in the browser long. A lot can be done manualy, but there are also some well-known third-party that provide secure help in this actions, which can make sure that nothing is overseen, and we are more than happy to use them.
